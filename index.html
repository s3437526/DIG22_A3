About Us

Meet Harrison and Jeff Trigwell.

The Brothers and business partners are both qualified and experienced architects, with the firm having worked on a number of major projects in the Fremantle area and surrounding suburbs. They have been operating for over 15 years as Trigwell architects and pride themselves in the level of quality product they have produced.

Their portfolio includes both residential and commercial projects. Most recently they designed the head office building for international shipping company Brighton & Swift Co.

Trigwell architects specialise in high-end commercial and residential design.
